Are you offline?

Windows
  1. Go to the taskbar(The bar at the bottom)
  2. Go to the left part, there should be multiple icons
  3. If you see then you are offline
  4. If you see then your computer can't find any networks, try restarting your computer.
  5. You can still use Leap! whilst offline, you just wont be able to go to any of the search results
MacOS
  1. Go to your status bar (at the very top)
  2. Go to the left part, there should be multiple icons
  3. If you see an icon like
  4. If you see , or you're offline
  5. If you see or it could be your WiFi or a server outage in Leap!
  6. If step 5 applies to you then attempt connecting to a different WiFi or checking the device you're connected to
